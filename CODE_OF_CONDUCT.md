# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers of **ciph** pledge to make participation in this project a respectful, inclusive, and harassment‑free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio‑economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

---

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

* Being respectful and considerate in communication
* Using welcoming and inclusive language
* Accepting constructive criticism gracefully
* Focusing on what is best for the community and the project
* Showing empathy toward other community members

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

* Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech
* Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
* Personal or political attacks
* Publishing others’ private information without consent
* Sustained disruption of discussions

---

## Security‑Focused Community Expectations

Because **ciph** is a cryptography and security‑focused project:

* Discussions should remain technical and evidence‑based
* Disagreements must be handled professionally
* Claims about cryptography should be backed by references or reasoning
* Fear‑mongering, misinformation, or irresponsible disclosure will not be tolerated

---

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and enforcing this Code of Conduct. They have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct.

---

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

* Do **not** engage publicly
* Report the incident privately

## Enforcement Guidelines

Maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate, including:

* Warning the individual
* Temporary or permanent bans
* Removing content
* Blocking access to the repository

The severity of the response will be proportional to the violation.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces, including:

* GitHub issues
* Pull requests
* Discussions
* Commit messages
* Any official communication related to the project

The goal of this Code of Conduct is not restriction, but collaboration.

Strong opinions are welcome. Disrespect is not.
